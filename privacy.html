PunchFall Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

PunchFall is published by Alkorg Studio. This policy explains how the app uses data and device permissions.

Camera Access

PunchFall uses the front camera to enable real-time gameplay and hand tracking. Camera and motion analysis are performed on-device. PunchFall does not upload or store your camera footage.

Game Center

If you use leaderboards, PunchFall uses Apple Game Center. Game Center may process your player identity, scores, rankings, and related leaderboard activity through Apple’s services.

Remote App Configuration

PunchFall may contact a remote service to fetch app-version and update configuration, such as minimum supported version, latest version, update messaging, and App Store destination links.

Apple Watch Companion

If enabled, PunchFall may exchange status and control information with its Apple Watch companion app using Apple platform services between your paired devices.

Data Storage

PunchFall stores gameplay-related settings and progression data locally on your device, such as preferences, scores, streaks, unlocked badges, and theme selection.

Children’s Privacy

PunchFall is not intended to collect personal information from children through its own backend systems. If you use Apple services such as Game Center, those services are governed by Apple’s policies.
