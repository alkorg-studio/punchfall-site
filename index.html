About

PunchFall is a fast AR boxing fitness game built for quick, score-driven training sessions. Punch falling targets, chase combos, and compete on Game Center leaderboards.

Features

  • Front-camera boxing gameplay with on-device hand tracking
  • Classic, Survival, Time Attack, and Daily Challenge modes
  • Game Center leaderboards
  • Unlockable themes, badges, and progression
  • Apple Watch companion support
