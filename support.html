PunchFall Support

If you need help with PunchFall, contact:

alkorgstudio.support@gmail.com

Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

Common Topics

  • Camera access is required for gameplay
  • Game Center sign-in is required for leaderboard participation
  • Apple Watch features require a paired watch and installed companion app

Troubleshooting

  • Check that camera permission is enabled in iPhone Settings
  • Make sure Game Center is signed in if leaderboards are unavailable
  • Restart the app if the camera preview or tracking does not initialize correctly
